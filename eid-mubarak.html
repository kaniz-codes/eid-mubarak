Takbir & Dua

اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ، اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ، اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ،
لَا إِلَهَ إِلَّا اللَّهُ، وَاللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ،
اللَّهُ أَكْبَرُ وَلِلَّهِ الْحَمْدُ
Allāhu Akbar, Allāhu Akbar, Allāhu Akbar,
Lā ilāha illā Allāh, wa Allāhu Akbar,
Allāhu Akbar wa lillāhi l-ḥamd
Allah is the Greatest, Allah is the Greatest, Allah is the Greatest.
There is no god but Allah, and Allah is the Greatest.
Allah is the Greatest, and to Allah belongs all praise.

This Takbir is recited in the days leading up to and on Eid, beginning after the Maghrib on the eve of Eid.
